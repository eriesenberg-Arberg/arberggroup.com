Investments

Patient capital.
Private conviction.

Arberg Group deploys its own capital — not third-party funds — into private operating companies and alternative assets selected for long-term durability.

We invest as owners,
not as managers
of other people's money.

Our investment activity is private, selective, and relationship-driven. We do not publish a deal list, accept unsolicited pitches, or operate as a fund of any kind.

When we commit capital, we commit for the long term. We look for businesses with durable fundamentals, honest operators, and clear paths to value creation — and we back them with patience.

Investment principles
Own capital only

We invest the family's own capital. No outside LPs, no fund structure, no mandated return timelines.

Private markets

Our focus is exclusively private — operating companies and alternative assets where relationships and diligence create an edge.

Conviction over volume

We make fewer bets with deeper conviction. We are not a diversified vehicle — we are a focused one.
